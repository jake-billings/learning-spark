LearningSpark

LearningSpark's goal is to facilitate the education of all students in science, technology, engineering, and math via high quality lessons delivered in our custom boxes.

  • Educate Faster

What can Learning Spark do?

Founded as part of the MIT Launch Competition, LearningSpark is a K-8 STEM education startup focused on bridging the student resource gap through subscription boxes. As a team of six, we qualified as national finalists and presented our idea at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology.

Our Team
